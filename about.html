About The Seven Bridges

The Seven Bridges is a joint partnership between Dance City and the West End Refugee Service (WERS). Nested inside a vibrant and inclusive venue. The Seven Bridges offers great food, promotes diversity, strengthens community connections and offers placements to empower volunteers within a supportive environment.

Collaborating with the Head Chef, the menu is thoughtfully crafted and updated regularly to highlight the diverse techniques and traditions of our dedicated volunteers. Our aim is to showcase their rich culinary contributions and provide a platform for them to enhance their existing skills, acquire new ones, and gain valuable experience that serves as a stepping stone towards future employment opportunities.
