About

The Seven Bridges is a joint partnership between Dance City and the West End Refugee Service (WERS). Nested inside a vibrant and inclusive space. The Seven Bridges offers great food, promotes diversity, strengthens community connections and offers placements to empower volunteers within a supportive environment.

The idea

Collaborating with the Head Chef, the menu is being thoughtfully crafted and updated regularly to highlight the diverse techniques and traditions of our dedicated volunteers. Our aim is to not only showcase their rich culinary contributions but also provide a platform for them to enhance existing skills, acquire new ones and gain valuable experience that serves as a stepping stone towards future employment opportunities.

Volunteer

In this 6-week placement, our volunteers will:

  • gain experience of working in a professional café
  • receive one to one support from the Head Chef
  • access training for Level 2 Food Hygiene and Safety Certificate
  • introduce their favourite food to the public
  • benefit from travel expenses and free meals on-duty
